Destinations we travel to

Southern and East Africa are quite easily some of the best safari and holiday destinations in the world. Johannesburg is the regional airport hub which feeds into all these neighbouring countries.

Tanzania in East Africa you can fly directly into Kilimanjaro International airport, and also easily across to Zanzibar.

Most of these countries have a very limited access to Kosher, due to dwindling or non existent Jewish populations. However using our contacts in the hotels and lodges, we have a solution for those Kosher clients who want to venture further out of South Africa, using our contacts and are happy to perhaps have a slightly limited selection of Kosher variety in terms of their meals.

Our backstory

Our expert team has been helping travelers from around the world plan and book high-end safaris for over 23 years. With roots going back to Lithuania, Germany, Poland and the UK, in the previous 2 centuries, some of our surviving family found their way to South Africa after the 2nd World War and spent time exploring and working in national parks all over the continent.

Out of this was birthed our family owned business, that focusses on Kosher safari experiences that are luxurious and private. This enhances your flexibility ten fold, compared to an open group tour with a set itinerary.

Families with children and grandchildren much prefer this private style of travel at their own pace. When you book with us, you'll also benefit from our industry expertise and receive preferential rates from safari lodges. Add another layer of relationships that we have spread across the entire continent, from Jewish doctors who live on the slopes of Kilimanjaro in Tanzania, to Rabbi's who bring their ornate Sefer Torah's with to a family reunion at a safari lodge outside Kruger, to Kosher chefs, who have a love for making incredible Glatt meals on the banks of the Zambezi river near Victoria Falls.

We hope you enjoy browsing our website, and feel free to contact us when you are ready to plan your ultimate kosher safari.

Group travel

When it comes to arranging your perfect Kosher Group Safari tour in Southern and East Africa, often the logistical planning becomes a bit tricky due to the fact that these areas, in many cases, do not have much of a permanent Jewish population living nearby.

Senior Travel

We have many retired couples wanting to experience a good quality safari, with top class service, and yet also expect to experience world class service. These lodges tend to have a handful of rooms, offer very high levels of personalised service, and attention to detail.

Family Travel

We are very experienced and focussed on building your perfect small family unit journey with mom, dad and their own children, or your larger multi generational family trip with 3 or even 4 generations travelling together. We are experts at taking the difficult planning out of these trips, and have earned our stripes ten times over with large families.

Tailor-made travel kosher safaris

One of the major focus points of our company is the utmost flexibility. In fact, 90% of our clients chose not to take a fixed program. They rather approach us, to listen intently to their needs, their set of dates, consider the options, be educated by our local experts, to the point of making an informed decision, and then finally deciding on a trip that is tailored to suit their needs.
